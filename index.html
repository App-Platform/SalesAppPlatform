Ultimate Business Card App

The Ultimate Business Card App is an unlimited, track-able, business card that functions as a never ending referral source for you and your business. Whether you or a referral partner are passing your information, you will now see who gave out your information and you'll always get the email address of the person to whom you are being recommended. This empowers you to follow up immediately and be in control of the entire referral process. You will no longer be waiting for referrals to contact you off a business card you weren't aware was given out.

Our Ultimate Business Card App includes:

  • A page with simple instructions on loading your app
  • Your app on Google Play Marketplace
  • One page for your information
  • One custom email referral link
  • One custom map link
  • Custom header logos
  • Custom loading page
  • Custom smart phone icons
  • User up datable photo gallery

All of our Apps are added to Google Play (the Android Market) and we create a landing page on your website (or on App-Platform.net) with QR codes and instructions on how to load the app on whatever device your customer has.

Custom Info Capture App

Capturing information accurately is crucial in today's fast-paced business world. Our Custom Info Capture App makes the process quick and easy. A pen and paper form is fine, but what if you could be sure all the required information you needed was legible, accurate, and archived electronically immediately after it was recorded? You can with our Custom Info Capture App! We will build your app with up to three sections and include up to 25 custom fields for one low price, including validation of up to 5 required fields.

Capture data from prospective clients while in your office or in the field. Capture information at your next trade show or buying show. Use it as an easy way to to show off your product or services and 'wow' your customers.

Of course we recommend having both our Ultimate Business Card App and the Custom Info Capture App. But if you'd like only one App and need to be able to collect data on the go, we can add a custom email referral link and contact information to your Custom Info Capture App for another $50.

FAQ's

Q: Why do I need an App?
A: Think of it as another tool to help your business. Just as having a website became a necessity, so too will mobile / web applications. Our flagship Unlimited Business Card App is the perfect referral tool for your business.

Q: Does the person I want to send my info to have to have a smart phone?
A: Nope! Only the person sending the info has to have the smart phone.

Q: What smart phones / tablets work?
A: Devices running iOS (iPhone, iPad and iPod touch), Android devices (Droid, Nexus, etc.), Windows Phones and newer Blackberry devices. Pretty much anything that has a web browser will work!

Q: Why do I need hosting?
A: To make the app as easy as possible to distribute we create a webpage with instructions on loading the app on various devices. Also, some smart phones will use a web version of the app and information has to "live" somewhere.

Q: What the heck is a subdomain?
A: It is a prefix to a web address, for example YourApp.app-platform.net.

Q: Will my app have an icon or do I get that stupid bookmark icon?
A: Your app will have custom icons on iDevices, Android devices, and windows phones!

Q: Will my app be in the Google Play Store (Android Market)?
A: Yes! We submit all of our apps to Google so your app will be searchable.

Q: Will my app be in the iTunes store?
A: Unfortunately submitting apps to the iTunes store is costly and can take a very long time. However, our web app functions exactly like an app from iTunes with custom icons and loading screens. If you would like to submit your app to Apple App Store yourself, we will compile an iTunes friendly version of your app at no additional charge.
